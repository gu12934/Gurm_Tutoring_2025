My Teaching Journey

September 2020 - Present

TutorDoctor - Private Tutor

Position: Math and Sciences Tutor

Focus: All types of students, primarily math and sciences

At TutorDoctor, I work with private tutors who understand the importance of each child's education. I help students navigate their educational journey, providing them with the building blocks they need to succeed while helping shape the person they become. Whether students are discovering who they are, making new friends, or handling academic challenges, I provide personalized support for their unique experiences.

Recognition: Received "Tutor of the Month" award for exceptional student outcomes and dedication.

September 2021 - Present

MakerKids Leaside - Coding Instructor

Position: Weekend Coding Instructor

Focus: STEM education for children aged 6-14

I love teaching code to kids! At MakerKids Leaside, I work in a maker space and educational program offering STEM programs and workshops. I help students develop problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity through hands-on, project-based learning.

Subjects Covered: Coding, Robotics, 3D Printing, Electronics, Game Design

Programs: After-school programs, camps, workshops, and birthday parties

April 2019 - December 2021

Kumon Inc. - Math & Reading Instructor

Position: Instructor (nearly 3 years)

Focus: K-12 Math and Reading

Started as a Kumon student myself and transitioned to become an instructor. The Kumon method emphasizes self-learning and individualized instruction through a structured, step-by-step approach. I provided daily practice support and weekly center sessions, helping students progress at their own pace.

Personal Note: I really enjoyed teaching and talking about Pokemon cards with students after they completed tests! This experience taught me the importance of connecting with students beyond just academics.

February 2019 - April 2020

Calgary Bridge Foundation for Youth

Position: Volunteer NxtGen Tutor/Event Coordinator & Parks Program Facilitator

Focus: Supporting immigrant and refugee youth integration

CBFY is a non-profit organization helping immigrant and refugee youth and their families integrate into Canadian society. I contributed to community events including their Annual Awards Ceremony and Community Day at Village Square Library.

Roles:

  • NxtGen Tutor for math and sciences (high school students)
  • Parks Program Facilitator designing lesson plans and physical education activities for elementary students
  • Event coordination and retreat facilitation

Impact: Helped provide settlement, education, and well-being support to immigrant and refugee youth in Calgary.

2020 - Present

Preply - Online Math Tutor

Position: Independent Online Tutor

Rating: 5.0/5.0 stars based on 6 student reviews

Lessons Completed: 518+ sessions

Teaching high school and university level mathematics online through Preply platform. I specialize in interactive teaching methods using Google Jamboard and focus on real-world applications to help students build confidence.

Specializations: Elementary Math, High School Math, University Calculus, IB Math, AP Calculus, Trigonometry, Functions, Pre-calculus

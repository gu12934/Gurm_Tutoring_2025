How to Create a Google Calendar Invite

Follow these step-by-step instructions to send me a calendar invite for our tutoring session:

Step 1: Open Google Calendar

Go to calendar.google.com and sign in to your Google account. If you don't have a Google account, you'll need to create one first.

Step 2: Create a New Event

Click the "+" button or "Create" button, usually located in the top-left corner of the screen. Alternatively, you can click on any time slot in the calendar to create an event.

Step 3: Add Event Title

In the event title field, enter something descriptive like:

  • "Math Tutoring Session with Gurmol"
  • "Science Tutoring - [Your Subject] with Gurmol"
  • "Calculus Help Session with Gurmol Singh"

Step 4: Set Date and Time

Select your preferred date and time for the tutoring session. Consider:

  • Your timezone (make sure it's set correctly)
  • Duration of the session (typically 1-2 hours)
  • Allow buffer time before and after

Step 5: Add My Email

In the "Add guests" or "Guests" section, type: gurmolsofly12@gmail.com

This will automatically send me an invitation to the tutoring session.

Step 6: Add Session Details

In the description field, please include:

  • Your Grade Level: (e.g., Grade 11, University Year 2)
  • Subject: (e.g., Math 30-1, Calculus, Chemistry)
  • Specific Topics: (e.g., Quadratic Functions, Derivatives, Stoichiometry)
  • Learning Goals: What you want to achieve in the session
  • Homework/Materials: Any specific homework or textbook chapters

Step 7: Set Meeting Location/Method

Specify how we'll meet:

  • Online: Add "Google Meet" or "Zoom" (I'll send the link)
  • Platform: Mention if you prefer Preply platform
  • In-Person: If local Calgary area, specify preferred location

Step 8: Add Google Meet (Optional)

For online sessions, you can:

  • Click "Add Google Meet video conferencing"
  • This automatically generates a meeting link for our session
  • Or mention that you'd prefer me to set up the meeting link

Step 9: Set Notification Preferences

Add reminders for yourself:

  • 15-30 minutes before the session
  • 1 day before for preparation
  • This helps you prepare materials in advance

Step 10: Send the Invitation

Click "Save" or "Send" to create the event and send me the calendar invitation. I'll receive an email notification and can confirm our session!

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to booking requests?

I usually respond within 6 hours of receiving a calendar invite or email. For urgent requests, please mention "URGENT" in the subject line.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem! Just send me an updated calendar invite or email at least 24 hours before the scheduled session when possible.

Do you provide materials for sessions?

Yes! I create personalized notes using Google Jamboard during our sessions, and I provide additional resources and practice problems as needed.

Can parents join the session?

Absolutely! Parents are welcome to join sessions, especially for younger students. Just let me know in advance so I can adjust my teaching approach accordingly.

My Tutoring Journey

From Student to Teacher

My journey began as a Kumon student, where I completed math through grade 9 and reading through grade 8. This experience taught me the value of discipline and good study habits. Even though I initially dreaded going to classes every Tuesday and Thursday after school, I secretly enjoyed my time there and learned to appreciate the structured learning approach.

This personal experience as a student gives me unique insight into the challenges students face and helps me connect with them on a deeper level. I understand what it's like to struggle with difficult concepts and the satisfaction that comes from finally mastering them.

In April 2019, I transitioned from being a Kumon student to becoming an instructor myself. I had actually applied in 2016 but wasn't accepted because it wasn't the right time. When I re-applied in 2019, my boss was impressed by my persistence and determination to keep trying.

Study Tips & Learning Strategies

Interactive Learning

Use visual aids like Google Jamboard to work through problems step-by-step. Drawing and visualizing concepts helps reinforce understanding and memory retention.

Consistent Practice

Regular, short study sessions are more effective than cramming. Set aside dedicated time each day for math and science practice to build strong foundations.

Ask Questions

Never hesitate to ask questions, no matter how basic they might seem. Understanding the "why" behind concepts is just as important as knowing the "how".

Real-World Applications

Connect mathematical concepts to real-world scenarios. This makes learning more meaningful and helps with retention and application of knowledge.
